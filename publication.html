A4

The A4 size is a commonly used paper size for writing paper, stationery, cards and documents. The size A4 reflects the size of a single sheet of paper. A4 is the default paper size for a reason. It is the most common paper size for business documents, school reports, fliers, menus, and door signs. As the name of its North American equivalent suggests, it's also the most common size for letters. If you plan on scanning your document to another person or machine, A4 size is your best bet.

A3

Ledger/Tabloid paper, and its closest ISO paper size equivalent A3, is often used in record keeping. Wills, deeds, and other important financial transactions are typically printed on ledger paper, whose slightly heavier weight gives it a higher degree of durability and permanence. Leger Paper is also used for small posters, flyers, and brochures.
